Begin Again (2014)

In this charming comedy from the director of ONCE, a hapless record producer discovers a talented young singer in a local bar still reeling from a breakup with her rock-star boyfriend. Somewhere between friendship and their love of music, the two strangers strike a chord that captures the hearts of everyone around them, proving that every great story has its own soundtrack.

Director: John Carney
Writer: John Carney
Cast:
  • Keira Knightly
  • James Corden
  • Mark Ruffalo
  • CeeLo Green
  • Hailee Steinfeld
  • Catherine Keener
  • Adam Levine

The Good Lie (2014)

Inspired by true events, THE GOOD LIE follows a young refugee of the Sudanese Civil War who, with three other Lost Boys, wins a lottery for relocation to the United States. Upon their arrival to America, they are introduced to a social worker, who learns about their hardships and teaches them how to navigate the modern world. They maintain their sincerity and earnestness in the face of major culture shock, but the young man struggles to resolve his feelings of guilt about the brother he left behind.

Director: Philippe Falardeau
Writer: Margaret Nagle
Cast:
  • Reese Witherspoon
  • Corey Stoll
